## Users

LedgerAI serves three overlapping user groups across Indian businesses, all on desktop:

- **SME business owners** managing their own books: non-accountants who need clarity without jargon. They check in daily or weekly to see if things are on track and compliant.
- **Professional Chartered Accountants (CAs)** handling multiple client books: power users who need speed, density, and trust in the numbers. They work in focused sessions, often during filing deadlines.
- **Finance teams at mid-sized corporates**: collaborative users who need audit trails, compliance confidence, and exportable reports for stakeholders.

All users share one context: seated at a desk, focused, working with sensitive financial data. Trust and security are non-negotiable.

## Product Purpose

LedgerAI is an AI-native service platform that delivers accounting, compliance, and financial intelligence services directly to Indian businesses. It replaces fragmented workflows (manual bookkeeping, separate compliance tools, CA consultations) with a single intelligent surface that understands Indian Accounting Standards (Ind AS), GST, and TDS natively.

Success looks like: a business owner or CA opens LedgerAI and within seconds knows their compliance status, can generate any statutory report, and gets AI-powered guidance that rivals a senior CA's advice.

## Brand Personality

**Trustworthy. Easy. Professional.**

LedgerAI speaks with the calm authority of a seasoned chartered accountant, not the hype of a startup. It earns trust through precision, not decoration. Every interaction should feel like working with someone who knows exactly what they're doing.

Reference: **Linear** — minimal, fast, professional. Information-dense without feeling heavy. Every pixel serves a purpose.

## Anti-references

- **Not a generic Tally clone.** No dated UI patterns, no spreadsheet-as-interface, no legacy visual language.
- **Not a toy-ish startup dashboard.** No oversized illustrations, no gamification, no "fun" at the expense of credibility.
- **Not overly colorful like consumer apps.** Color is used with restraint and purpose, not for decoration or visual noise.

## Design Principles

1. **Earn trust through precision.** Every number, label, and status indicator must feel deliberate and verifiable. Ambiguity erodes trust in financial software.
2. **Reduce cognitive load, not capability.** Make complex accounting accessible without dumbing it down. A CA and a first-time business owner should both feel served.
3. **Security is visible.** Users handling sensitive financial data need to feel the security, not just know it exists. Authentication, session handling, and data boundaries should be evident in the experience.
4. **Speed is respect.** Financial professionals work under deadline pressure. Every interaction should feel instant and every workflow should minimize steps.
5. **Quiet confidence over loud features.** Let the intelligence surface naturally. AI assistance should feel like a trusted colleague, not a chatbot performing tricks.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target: WCAG 2.1 AA (to be refined as the product matures)
- Ensure sufficient contrast ratios across the dark theme, especially for financial data and compliance status indicators
- Support keyboard navigation for all core workflows (transaction entry, report generation, compliance review)
- Consider color-blind-safe palettes for status indicators (error/warning/success) — critical for compliance dashboards
